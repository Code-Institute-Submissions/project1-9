Explore the South Kerry Greenway

Live the dream and Explore the Greenway

Explore T&C here.

The proposed route of the greenway is approximately 32km in length. The greenway is broadly aligned with the corridor of the abandoned Great Southern and Western Railway from Reenard, west of Caherciveen, to Glenbeigh in South West Kerry.

A Greenway cycleway is defined as a public road or proposed public road reserved for the exclusive use of cyclists or cyclists and pedestrians. A greenway as proposed herein is a cycleway that caters for pedestrian and cyclists in a recreational environment1
